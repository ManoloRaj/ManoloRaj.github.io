STORY OF MY FAMILY

  • by:Manolo RAJAONAH
  • date:March 31, 2020
  • 3 Comments

I am proud to be Malagasy, many people thing that nowadays keep remind our origin is no really important. . Someone think that career, glory, and fame are enough. For me, life is not like that, you can not achieve anything until you know where and why are you from ? know where you came from to know where would you go. That is the most important things as you , as your humanity.

I know that I am from Madagascar but I also know that I am from Antananarivo or litteraly the "City of paradise". Malagasy culture is so beautiful, I love what wonderful mindset my ancestor though about create a city where everything was about fraternity, mindset of helping each other in happy times and much more in bad times.

More story next time but I will send you my Family tree then, it contain more than 10 generations of mine ancestors.
